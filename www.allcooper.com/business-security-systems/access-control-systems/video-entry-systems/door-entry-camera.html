What is a Door Entry Camera?

Door entry cameras are the fundamental part of your video intercom system (or camera intercom). 

The door camera allows you to identify visitors, easily, from a safe location.

Whilst viewing the door camera, the door entry intercom allows you to converse with the visitor and determine whether they are entitled to access.

Door entry systems provide simple security

Choosing the right door entry camera

There are many different models on the market, but typically, a camera door entry system will provide high quality colour images, so you can clearly see who wishes to gain entry to your property.
 

The system should also include one or two-way speakers, so you can hear and speak to a visitor from a location inside (such as a reception area).

For ease of movement, wireless door entry systems are also available that include portable devices as part of the system. For example, a wireless handset included with the system can allow you to speak to the person at the door from wherever you are in the building.
